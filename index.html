Welcome to the

SCUT Multimedia Technology and Image Processing Research Lab

Our Lab at South China University of Technology is directed by Professors Xuemiao Xu. We are a group of researchers with shared interests in multimedia technology and image processing research. We work on a variety of topics including computer graphics, computer vision, pattern recognition, image processing.

About us

Our research addresses the practical applications of computational vision. We are focused on discovering and proposing a variety of topics including computer graphics, computer vision, pattern recognition, image processing. At the same time, our curiosity leads us to made good progress in the research of visual recognition, graphic image processing, and its application in the fields of intelligent transportation, public safety, and intelligent manufacturing. Our lab has published more than 30 papers in international important journals (TOG, TVCG, Sensors, etc.) and conferences (SIGGRAPH, SIGGRAPH Asia, NPAR, CVM, etc.).

Join The Lab

We are always looking for outstanding prospective students. Our goal is to help every lab member thrive in the lab and fulfill his or her maximum potential. If you are interested in joining our lab, please feel free to send an email to xuemx@scut.edu.cn to introduce yourself.
